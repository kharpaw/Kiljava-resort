Past and Present


During the Ice Age, a massive ice block pressed into Kiljava's ridge, creating Finland's largest spring called Sääksjärvi.
The water is uniquely crystal clear and was once believed to have healing properties. This led to the establishment of various
facilities such as sanatoriums in the area.
Kiljava Resort’s private lakeside sauna is located right on the shore of this spring, and some rooms also offer tranquil
lake views of Sääksjärvi.
According to legend, Nurmijärvi's own Aleksis Kivi used Sääksjärvi as inspiration for Ilvesjärvi in *Seven Brothers.*
Kiljava Resort’s premises have a rich history as a folk high school, extending to the golden era of President Urho Kekkonen.
SAK has traditionally held its annual meetings here, and the lakeside sauna has hosted several of our time’s presidents.
Kiljava’s history and environment speak for themselves and are best experienced in person!
