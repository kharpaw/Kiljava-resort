Private Events

All events with high quality and comfort



TAILOR-MADE

Kiljava Resort is a versatile and comfortable meeting and conference hotel where you can host a smooth meeting or memorable parties with accommodation and catering. We have a space suitable for every kind of event, from small gatherings to events for up to 400 people.

We are happy to assist you with planning the event and acquiring any additional services.

You can submit a request for a quote for your event using this form, and we will contact you.











KOTA

* Kota is a grill hut suitable for both summer and winter use, with windows and a terrace offering an idyllic lake view. Kota is an excellent choice for relaxed gatherings and wilderness-inspired cooking.

* Food and drinks are delivered to the event through the hotel.

* Maximum group size: 20 people.

RESERVE

SWIMMING POOL WITH SAUNAS

* The swimming pool (17 m) and saunas can also be reserved for private events.

* Separate saunas for men and women.

* Outdoor hot tub.

RESERVE

FIREPLACE ROOM

* The cozy fireplace room overlooks the swimming pool and is, of course, at its coziest with a fire burning in the hearth.

* Maximum group size: 20 people.

RESERVE

DINING HALL

* The dining hall, which can be adapted for various events, can also have a stage with sound equipment. Host your unforgettable wedding here or book it for your own company!

* Private bar and performance stage.

* Maximum group size: 150 people.

RESERVE

CABINETS

* Several fully equipped cabinets are perfect for organizing various meetings and events.

* Group size ranging from five people up to 62 people.

RESERVE

AUDITORIUM

* Your company's training event is at its best in our fully equipped auditorium.

* The auditorium has 120 seats.

RESERVE

PRIVATE BEACH SAUNA

* A private beach sauna on the shores of the crystal-clear Sääksjärvi lake. Book the sauna for your event or enjoy a fun evening with friends!

* Separate saunas for men and women, as well as a shared fireplace room.

* Food and drinks are delivered to the event through the hotel.

* Maximum group size: 30 people.

RESERVE

GYM HALL

* Book our facilities for your company or association's annual meeting, or organize an intense sports weekend for your team!

* We rent the gym hall to sports clubs, and the hall can also be transformed into a conference or event space for up to 400 people.

* Possible sports: badminton, volleyball, basketball, futsal, and floorball.
