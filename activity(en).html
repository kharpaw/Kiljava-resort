Activities

At Kiljava Resort, you'll find a variety of activities for everyone. The crystal-clear Lake Sääksjärvi is worth seeing, and the surrounding beautiful nature is a must-experience.




In Summer

During the summer, staying at the hotel becomes even more enjoyable. The following activities are available during summer:
– Gym
– Swimming
– Canoeing
– Running on trails
– Fatbike
– Frisbee Golf
– Futsal
– Archery
– Tennis
– Floorball
– Badminton
– Basketball
– Volleyball
All these activities can be enjoyed during summer with friends or family in the fresh summer weather.




In Winter

Winter may seem like a time with fewer activities, but we have plenty to keep you entertained during your stay. Here's what you can enjoy in winter:
– Gym
– Ice Swimming
– Swimming in the pool
– Skiing
– Futsal
– Floorball
– Badminton
– Basketball
– Volleyball
Enjoy staying active with us even in the lovely cold winter season.
