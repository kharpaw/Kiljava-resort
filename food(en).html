Dining

Enjoy the breakfast buffet, lunch buffet, and café bar offerings at Kiljava Resort.



Opening Hours

Hotel breakfast is included in the room rate and is served Monday to Friday from 08:00 to 09:30 and on weekends from 08:00 to 10:00. The breakfast buffet is available to non-resident guests for €13.00.

The lunch buffet is available at Restaurant Kiljava Monday to Friday from 11:00 to 13:00 for €15.00, and Bar Kiljava is open during reception hours, where light snacks are also available.

Note! Opening hours and lunch services may change according to the seasons. Availability can be confirmed at the reception.
